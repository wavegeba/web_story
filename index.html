Founder

Dr. Geba

Dr. Geba is a multidisciplinary expert with more than 20 years of experience across electrical engineering, environmental engineering, remote sensing, GeoAI, and data science. His work focuses on developing practical geospatial intelligence for agricultural and environmental applications.

His recent work has been recognized through the GeoGov Summit Achievement in Geospatial Research Award for advancing SAR–optical interoperability and improving the reliability of Earth observation for agricultural monitoring.

Her approach emphasizes empathy, trust-building, constructive dialogue, and collaborative problem solving across personal, organizational, and multicultural contexts.

GeoGov Summit Achievement in Geospatial Research Award

GEBA’s founding research was recognized for addressing a long-standing challenge in agricultural monitoring: how rainfall distorts radar signals and complicates vegetation mapping.

The work developed a practical framework to understand and correct precipitation effects on SAR-based vegetation indicators, improving SAR–optical interoperability and strengthening reliable Earth observation for agricultural monitoring and food-security applications.
