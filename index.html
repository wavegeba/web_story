About GEBA & Contact

GEBA LLC develops practical GeoAI solutions that transform Earth observation data into actionable intelligence for agriculture, environmental monitoring, sustainability, and collaborative decision-making.

Dr. Geba

Dr. Geba is a GeoAI scientist and remote sensing researcher with more than two decades of experience in Earth observation, environmental analytics, and geospatial data science. His work focuses on transforming satellite observations into actionable intelligence for agriculture, environmental monitoring, and sustainability applications.

His research has been recognized internationally, including the GeoGov Summit Achievement in Geospatial Research Award for advancing interoperability between radar and optical Earth observation systems.

Mia is a doctoral researcher whose work centers on intercultural engagement, relationship-centered communication, and human development. Her approach emphasizes empathy, trust-building, constructive dialogue, and collaborative problem solving across personal, organizational, and multicultural contexts.

GeoGov Summit Achievement in Geospatial Research Award

GEBA’s founding research was recognized for addressing a long-standing challenge in agricultural monitoring: how rainfall distorts radar signals and complicates vegetation mapping.

The work developed a practical framework to understand and correct precipitation effects on SAR-based vegetation indicators, improving SAR–optical interoperability and strengthening reliable Earth observation for agricultural monitoring and food-security applications.
