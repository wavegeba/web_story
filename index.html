Founder

Geba Chang

Dr. Geba is a multidisciplinary expert with more than 21 years of experience across electrical engineering, environmental engineering, remote sensing, GeoAI, and data science. His work focuses on developing practical geospatial intelligence for agricultural and environmental applications.

His recent work has been recognized with the GeoGov Award – Innovation in Geospatial Research for advancing SAR–optical interoperability and improving the reliability of Earth observation for agricultural monitoring.

Co-Founder

Renah Kim

Mrs. Renah is a doctoral researcher whose work centers on intercultural engagement, relationship-centered communication, and human development. Drawing from extensive international and cross-cultural experience, she supports individuals, leaders, families, and organizations through coaching, mentoring, and Nonviolent Communication.

Her approach emphasizes empathy, trust-building, constructive dialogue, and collaborative problem solving across personal, organizational, and multicultural contexts.

GeoGov Award – Innovation in Geospatial Research

Dr. Geba was recognized for addressing a long-standing challenge in agricultural monitoring: how rainfall distorts radar signals and affects vegetation mapping accuracy.

The work developed a practical framework to understand and correct precipitation effects on SAR-based vegetation indicators, improving SAR–optical interoperability and strengthening reliable Earth observation for agricultural monitoring and food-security applications.
