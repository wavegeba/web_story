GeoAI & Data Science Internship

GEBA Internship Program

GEBA Internship Program is a practical 8–12 week learning experience offered during the July–October period for high school and university students interested in GeoAI, data science, remote sensing, coding, environmental intelligence, and real-world project development.

View 12-Week Program Internship History
12-Week Learning Path

From curiosity to real-world GeoAI projects

The GEBA internship introduces students to data thinking, coding practice, GeoAI concepts, remote sensing applications, and team-based project development through a 12-week learning structure. The program is designed to make data science approachable by connecting everyday questions, environmental observations, and practical coding exercises.
